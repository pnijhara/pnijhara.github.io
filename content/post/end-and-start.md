Writing this blog after a very long time. I was trying to complete my FastAPI journey and somewhat succeeded too. However, the aim was to create tutorials on each and every topic. Tried to create some of them. [Introduction to API, JSON and XML](https://pnijhara.github.io/2020/08/what-is-an-api-json-or-xml/) along with [HTTP methods and status codes](https://pnijhara.github.io/2020/08/http-methods-and-status-code/) went great. Areeb Jamal and Prateek Jain helped a lot in reviewing them. Some of the tutorials are still pending as drafts. I remember my last conversation with Areeb Bhai about the blogs. He used to push me a lot to write but I failed. Not because I was procrastinating but because tutorials need time and a huge amount of effort in which my college curriculum played the role of villain. Nevertheless, I will still try my best to get them to publish probably during the summer vacations. But for now, the plans are different.

From now on I will write my daily/weekly updates in the form of blogs. Some of them will surely go long as I will continue my semester reports also. This idea came after I read [Jason Braganza's blogs](https://janusworx.com/) on his journey of learning Django. His blogs are not tutorials but they are just recordings of his journey as he learns new things all over again.

So, from now on I will write everything I learn, everything I know about writing and everything else that I find interesting enough to share. Blogs from now on, won't be much longer. They will be just updates of everything I could grab in a day.
